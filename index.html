About Me

Hi, I’m Haresh. I’ve spent the last five years working as a Character and Graphic Designer, I’ve developed a unique approach that treats web portals as more than just visual spaces. I hold an MBA in Digital Marketing and I’m obsessed with the 'why' behind the design—how a visual identity or a character’s personality actually drives a marketing strategy forward. Beyond design, I have a background in performing editorial reviews to ensure that character narrative and layout work in harmony. I love bridging the gap between big-picture business goals and the creative execution that makes them happen. I’m currently looking to bring this mix of strategy and design to a team that values data-driven creativity.
